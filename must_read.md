## Tips for Reading Papers

1. **First Pass:** Read abstract, intro, conclusion
2. **Second Pass:** Understand main ideas and diagrams
3. **Third Pass:** Deep technical details
4. **Take Notes:** Always document key insights
5. **Implement:** Try implementing key ideas
6. **Discuss:** Share with others for deeper understanding
